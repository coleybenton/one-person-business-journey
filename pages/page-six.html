Four Pillars of a Six Figure One Person Business

Brand - You Are The Niche

Start with your goals, asking yourself: What do I want out of life? After that, the answer to the next question becomes clear - What are you leading people towards?

Content - Documenting Your Mind

Next, what's standing in the way of achieving your goals? We will consider this the problem/s. You can then use your experience with the problem to help guide others past those same roadblocks.

Product -Public Personal Projects

You can condense the solution you found to your problem into a step-by-step system for yourself. After creating your system, you can present it to others so they can solve their problems faster.

Marketing - Sell To Yourself

After overcoming the problem and achieving your goal, your life is now impacted in a positive way. Incorporate the details of those positive results in your message to let other people know that they can experience the same positive benefits you did.
